EPSILON2K25

A National Level Technical Symposium

Epsilon2k25 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) Department of Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College. This annual event brings together talented students from across the country to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation. It features a variety of technical and non-technical events, workshops, offering participants a chance to compete, learn, and network with industry experts. With a focus on cutting-edge technologies and real-world problem-solving, Epsilon2k25 serves as a hub for aspiring engineers to collaborate, explore new ideas, and push the boundaries of engineering excellence.

TECHNICAL

A technical symposium is an event organized by educational institutions to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, innovation, and knowledge. It includes paper presentations, project exhibitions, coding contests, workshops, and technical events, fostering learning, collaboration, and industry interaction.

NON-TECHNICAL

A Non-Technical Symposium is an event that focuses on fun, creativity, and skill-based activities rather than core technical knowledge. It includes events like IPL Auction, Quiz, Debate, Treasure Hunt, Gaming, and Creative Arts, encouraging teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving in an engaging and competitive environment.
